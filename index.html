Welcome to Fraudbusters: The CFO’s Gambit

How to Play

Section 1: Game Overview

1.1 Game Name: Fraudbusters: The CFO’s Gambit

1.2 Game Theme/Storyline: Players simulate the role of CFOs in a fintech company, managing investments, detecting fraud, and navigating market events to maximize the company’s Net Present Value (NPV). The game tests strategic decision-making and risk management.

1.3 Player Role: Players act as CFOs responsible for financial decision-making, fraud detection, and maximizing the company's value.

1.4 Number of Players: 3 players.

1.5 Number of Rounds (Years): 5 rounds (representing 5 years of business decisions).

1.6 Victory Criteria: The player with the highest cumulative NPV at the end of 5 rounds wins.

Section 2: Game Flow

In each round, you will be given two investment options. You will:

  • Choose one investment from the two options provided.
  • In the second step, choose a financing option: Debt, Equity, or Retained Earnings.

Repeat this process for 5 rounds, managing your investments and finances to maximize your company's NPV.

Section 3: How the Game Works

  • Investment Cards: At the start of each round, you will receive two investment options. You must evaluate each and select one to invest in.
  • Financing Method: Once you’ve selected your investment, you will choose how to finance it. Your options include Debt, Equity, or Retained Earnings.
  • Market Events: Each round may bring a market event that will affect the results of your investments, so plan carefully!
